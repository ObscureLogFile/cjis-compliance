## Access Control (AC)

### AC-1 - Policy and Procedures

**Purpose:** This control addresses policy and procedures for CJIS systems, users, or data.

**Procedure**

1. Identify the person, role, or system that needs access.
2. Obtain documented approval for the least privilege needed.
3. Provision the account, permissions, and MFA or other required controls.
4. Verify access works as intended and remove any extra privileges.

**Recommended approach**

Use centralized identity and access management, unique user accounts, and periodic reviews.

**Records / outputs**

- Control evidence, approval record, or log entry
- Exception or escalation record if needed
- Review note or attestation for the control cycle
